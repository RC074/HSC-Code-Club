About The Club


Hello, Welcome to the HSC Coding Club. Our main goal is to spark interest in those who are new to Computer Science, and to further develop the skills for the ones who are interested in coding. Hillfield Strathallan College has a great community and it is with this great community that we can further elevate the bar in the Computer Science/Coding segment. Currently, through our observations, we see a necessary place for a club dedicated to Computer Science. This will allow those who want to go above and beyond school/curriculum expectations and compete in the Coding competitions at a higher level helping the school not only achieve more, but also bring the community of Computer Scientists together in Hillfield. In addition, this club will enable more new students to learn and at least get a touch on Computer Science. As we think more towards the future, we see that computer science is becoming a popular, and exponentially growing segment in not only high school, but also going into university and work. This club will provide the support needed for those students who need extra help to become the top Computer Scientists of the future.
